Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 25, 2025

SatisSplit (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides a tool that helps you create and publish split‑screen videos formatted for TikTok. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Data we process

  • Account & OAuth data: when you connect your TikTok account, we receive an access token (and refresh token) scoped only to the permissions you approve (e.g., video.upload or video.publish).
  • Content files: videos you render locally. If you choose to upload from SatisSplit, the files are transmitted to TikTok via their API. We do not sell personal data.
  • Operational logs: non‑sensitive logs (timestamps, status codes, file sizes) to troubleshoot reliability.

How we use data

  • Authenticate your TikTok session and post drafts or direct posts per your request.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the service.
  • Provide support if you contact us.

Data retention

Access tokens are stored securely and rotated; you may revoke access at any time in TikTok’s settings. We keep operational logs for up to 30 days. We do not persist your source videos after upload unless you explicitly ask us to.

Your choices

  • Disconnect your TikTok account or revoke permissions in TikTok’s security settings.
  • Request data access or deletion by emailing support@satissplit.app or using the Data Deletion page.
  • Decline optional cookies (if a hosted web version adds analytics, we will present a banner and obtain consent where required).

International & legal

We follow applicable privacy laws (e.g., GDPR/CCPA) and process data on the basis of your consent and our legitimate interests in operating the service.
