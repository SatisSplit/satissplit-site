Data Deletion

Last updated: September 25, 2025

If you’d like us to delete account data (e.g., OAuth tokens, logs), email support@satissplit.app from the email associated with your account and include your TikTok username. We will confirm and complete deletion within 30 days, subject to legal obligations.

You may also revoke SatisSplit’s access to your TikTok account at any time in TikTok settings; this immediately disables posting from SatisSplit.
